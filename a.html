Welcome to Pets Info Page

This page gives some basic information about common pets.

1. Dog

Dogs are friendly animals. They are loyal and known as human's best friend.

2. Cat

Cats are cute and independent animals. They love to play and sleep a lot.

3. Rabbit

Rabbits are small and soft pets. They enjoy eating carrots and leafy vegetables.

4. Parrot

Parrots are colorful birds. Some parrots can even mimic human speech.

Note: Always take care of pets with love and provide them proper food and shelter.
